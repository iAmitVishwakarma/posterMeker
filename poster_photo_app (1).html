How It Works

TensorFlow.js

This app uses TensorFlow.js, a JavaScript library for machine learning, to analyze your images and detect the main subject with advanced neural networks.

BodyPix Model

We utilize the BodyPix model which can segment people and objects in real-time. It detects subjects to create an accurate mask for background removal.

Privacy Focused

All processing happens directly in your browser - your images never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. Your privacy is guaranteed.
